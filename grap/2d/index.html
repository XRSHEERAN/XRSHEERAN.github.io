This animation shows a balance and two earths, one happy earth and another sad earth. It represent the pollution
cost by car and other technology (for the assignment I drew a drone). Technically, this animation is hierarchical
The balance is drawn on the canvas and moving accordingly, it has two children: left plate and write plate, these two
plates move as balance rotates. Also the two plates each have their children; left: car and drone, right: tree and moon sun.
While car have its rotating wheels as children, drone has four rotating propellers and itself is rotating as well, the
tree has its moving arms and the rotating sun has moon rotating around it. We can also adjust rotating and scales
with the bar below.
